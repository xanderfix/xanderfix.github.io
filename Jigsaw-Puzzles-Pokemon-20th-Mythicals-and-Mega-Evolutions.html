Jigsaw Puzzles:
Pokemon 20th - Mythicals and Mega Evolutions

3/6/2016

I know it's been a while, but I finally got around to making some more digital jigsaw puzzles! As you may know, the 20th anniversary of the Pokemon series was last month, so I decided to make some Pokemon jigsaw puzzles to celebrate. If you want to adjust the puzzle's specs, you can click the gear in the top-right corner.

Mega Evolutions

You know what one of the best things about mega evolutions are? They take a really cool Pokemon, and make them look even more awesome! This first puzzle has every mega evolved 'mon (as of Gen 6 at least) from Alakazam to Venusuar, plus Primal Groudon and Kyogre.

2016 Mythical Pokemon

This year, to celebrate the 20th anniversary, The Pokemon Company has been giving out one mythical Pokemon every month. In case you don't know, mythical Pokemon are special 'mon similar to legendaries that can only be obtained through special real world distribution events. Obviously, the fact that 11 mythical Pokemon are being given out in one year (there wasn't one in January) is a huge deal. This second puzzle features all 11 of the mythical Pokemon being given out this year.

Gotta Catch 'Em All

If you want a challenge, then try this last puzzle: all 721 Pokemon organized by color (well, I didn't count, but there's at least 700 here). This is definitely the hardest puzzle I've posted so far, but if you complete it, then you can call yourself a Poke-puzzle master!

I hope you enjoy piecing together these Pokemon puzzles!
