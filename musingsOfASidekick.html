Musings of a Sidekick

9/6/2014

"Musings of a Sidekick" is a humorous article written to promote New Play Control! Pikmin 2. The article is written from the perspective of Louie, the secondary protagonist of the game. It compares Louie and the main protagonist, Captain Olimar, to other famous Nintendo dous like Mario and Luigi, and Donkey Kong and Diddy Kong. Though it's funnier if you've played Pikmin 2, any Nintendo fan will definetly find it quite amusing. You can read the article here.
