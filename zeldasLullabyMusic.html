How to Play Zelda's Lullaby(Ocarina of Time Version)

Here is the midi file, sheet music, and guitar tab for Zelda's Lullaby. The sheet music was made by importing the midi into a free program called Musescore, which you may need to download to open the sheet music. An amazing guitar tab for Zelda's Lullaby has already been created on the website csguitar89, so the link leads to that tab.
