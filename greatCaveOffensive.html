Kirby Super Star Ultra:
Great Cave Offensive Treasure References

9/30/2014

Many of the treasures in the Kirby Super Star/ Kirby Super Star Ultra game The Great Cave Offensive are refrences to other Nintendo games. Below is a table listing those treasures and the related refrence. As many treasures were changed to objects from other games in Kirby Super Star Ultra, the list is from that game.

Whip

A whip that strongly resembles the infamous Vampire Killer from the NES game Castlevania.

Screw Attack

The Screw Attack upgrade from the Metroid series. It was incorrectly labeled as Screw Ball in Kirby Super Star.

Phanto Mask

A Phanto is an enemy from Super Mario Bros. 2. This treasure was called the Zebra Mask in SUper Star because it looks like a zebra's nose.
