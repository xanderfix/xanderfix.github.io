How to Play the Mega Man Soundtrack

5/26/2016

After the completion of my Mega Man Piano Playthrough, I had MIDI files left over for every single song on the Mega Man soundtrack. I decided to touch up and release those MIDIs on the internet so that everyone can learn these amazing songs. You can click to download the MIDIs below, or watch a video from the Synthesia playlist. Have fun! (Note: the Wily stages and ending are currently unavailable, but I'm working hard to release those too. Check back over the next few days.)
