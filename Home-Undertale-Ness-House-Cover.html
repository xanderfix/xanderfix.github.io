Home (Undertale)/Ness's House Cover

7/26/2015

This is the first ever cover that I've done with another person, and also Nick's first on the YouTube channel! We've been working on this for months, and I'm very happy with the final result. The arrangement is a blend of "Home" from Undertale and "Home Sweet Home" from EarthBound (which in turn is a remix of "Pollyanna" from EarthBound Beginnings). Neither of us had done anything of the sort before, and we hope you enjoy it!
