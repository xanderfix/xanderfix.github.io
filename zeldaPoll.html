Which Zelda Game is Best?

9/9/2014

This is a poll to determine which Legend of Zelda game is the best. Remakes, spin-offs, and other non-canon games do not count. You may only vote once per week, per IP adress to keep the voting fair. The poll will run for one year. As a final note, the Wii U legend of Zelda will probably be released by the time this poll has ended, but does not count because it was not released prior to the poll's beginning.
